What Makes It Click

It hits that sweet spot where it's challenging enough to keep you hooked without making you want to throw your phone

  • No timers, no rushing - just pure puzzle-solving at your own pace.

  • New mechanics get introduced as you go, so the puzzles never get stale

  • The interface is pretty minimal - we didn't want a bunch of clutter messing with your focus

  • The levels really make you think ahead and plan your moves carefully
